Chicken Biryani

East or West hot Chicken Biryani is the best.

Chicken biryani is a popular dish in South Asian cuisine. It consists of spiced rice and chicken cooked together in layers. The dish is typically made with long-grain basmati rice and bone-in chicken pieces. It is flavored with a variety of aromatic spices and often served with raita, a yogurt-based condiment.

Ingredients üìù

  • 2 cups long-grain basmati rice, rinsed and drained
  • 1 pound bone-in chicken pieces, skin removed
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 medium onions, sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cups water
  • Chopped cilantro, for garnish

Instructions üç≤

  1. In a large bowl, mix the yogurt, ginger, garlic, cumin, coriander, turmeric, cinnamon, and salt. Add the chicken and mix well to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight.
  2. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the onions and cook until soft and translucent, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the onions with a slotted spoon and set aside.
  3. Add the chicken and its marinade to the pot and cook until the chicken is browned on all sides, about 5 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.
  4. Add the rice to the pot and stir to coat with the remaining oil and spices. Add the water and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 10-15 minutes or until the rice is almost cooked.
  5. Arrange the chicken on top of the rice in a single layer. Sprinkle the reserved onions over the chicken. Cover the pot and continue to cook over low heat until the rice is fully cooked and the chicken is tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  6. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for 5-10 minutes before uncovering. Fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with chopped cilantro before serving. Enjoy your delicious homemade chicken biryani!
